A Little Bit About Us...

The Lash Lab is a lash lift and tint provider in NW Calgary. Using Health Canada registered products to get the ultimate lift for your lashes!

Our process uses a two-in-one tint and lift system, cutting appointment times down by 70%. Our lifts can last up to 8 weeks with customizable results.

The Lash Lab is excited to be hitting the Calgary beauty industry with a revolutionalized instant lash lift system.
